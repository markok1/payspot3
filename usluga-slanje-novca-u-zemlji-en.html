Our services

SENDING MONEY IN THE COUNTRY - PAYSPOT NET

Fast connection, secure transfer!

For quick and easy money sending within Montenegro, there is PaySpot Net - your ideal option that enables quick and secure money transactions without the need for a recipient's bank account.

This service is especially intended for parents whose children study in other cities or for people who want to help their loved ones, regardless of the distance.

Money sent through PaySpot Net is available within minutes, allowing for immediate financial support when it's needed most. All you need to do is find your nearest PaySpot ME branch using our locator.

The process of sending money is simple - it is necessary to identify the Payer, provide a copy of the identification document AND provide the necessary information about the Recipient of the remittance, including name, surname, address and place. A TAN code (remittance code) is also assigned to the Payer and should be transmitted to the Recipient.

The payment of money is made in cash in the nominal amount at the branch of the Institution, with the identification of the Recipient and the presentation of the necessary data. Money transfer costs are borne by the Payer, in accordance with the Institution's tariff.
