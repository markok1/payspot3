Welcome to the payment institution PaySpot ME – your reliable partner in the world of payments in Montenegro!

The payment institution PaySpot ME is proud to be the first payment institution that provides innovative payment services at Montenegrin market. With our startup, we are revolutionizing the way people manage their finances.

Our mission is to revolutionize the way people make payments. We started with canvas traffic, but we are visionary looking ahead, preparing the ground for new services that will include money transfer in the country, international money transfer, market payment of legal entities and entrepreneurs, e-commerce, digital channels...

Regardless of the expansion of our services, our commitment to security remains unchanged. All our users can be sure that every transaction will be carried out with the highest standard of security.

Our vision is to be leaders in the transformation of payment transactions in Montenegro, creating innovative and affordable financial solutions that improve people's lives and support the country's economic development.
