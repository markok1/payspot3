Our services

RIA International Money Transfer
Fast and safe

PaySpot ME, in partnership with RIA Money Transfer, provides a fast, safe and reliable method of sending and receiving money worldwide. With over one billion successfully completed transactions, RIA ranks among the leading global leaders in money transfer.

Why choose RIA?

  • Speed - Money arrives for just a few minutes
  • Low Commissions – Affordable fees for every transfer
  • Security and Confidentiality - Protected data and a global branch network

How to send money?

Visit the PaySpot ME location

Visit the PaySpot ME location in Montenegro. Nearest location you can find it through the locator

Give basic data

Provide basic information: first and last name of the recipient, country and city, amount to send

Give money

Prepare the amount you want to pay

Share your send/transfer code

Share the PIN (sending/transfer code) with recipient for download money.

How to receive money through RIA?

Visit the PaySpot ME location

Visit the PaySpot ME location in Montenegro. Nearest location you can find it through the locator

Identification document

Bring a valid identification document

Communicate data

Provide the cashier with the following information: Name and surname sender, country and city from where the money was sent, Amount and PIN (sending/transfer code)

Get money

The money will be paid to you in full and in the specified currency at no additional cost

Track your transaction

  • Simple and fast! Check the status of yours transfers anytime via RIA online transaction tracking
  • Enter the transaction PIN (sending/transfer code) and find out where your money is

Important information:

  • Sender bears shipping costs
  • The recipient does not pay fees for receiving money
  • Do not share the PIN (sending/transfer code) with third parties security
