How to receive money through RIA?

Visit the RIA office

You can find information about the nearest RIA office at RIA site

Identification document

Bring a valid identification document

Communicate data

Provide the cashier with the following information: Name and surname sender, country and city from where the money was sent, Amount and PIN (sending/transfer code)

Get money

The money will be paid to you in full and in the specified currency at no additional cost
